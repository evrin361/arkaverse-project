# Phase 9.1 — Business Signals

Status: Complete

## Goal

Translate operational signals into business-oriented indicators.

## Implemented

### Business Signals Engine

Inputs:

* Runtime State
* Identity State
* Operational Signals

Outputs:

* System Readiness
* Engagement Level
* Operational Stability

### Dashboard Integration

Added Business Signals panel displaying:

* System Readiness
* Engagement Level
* Operational Stability

## Architecture

No new runtime layer introduced.

Business Signals are derived from existing Operational Signals.

## Outcome

ARKAVERSE now exposes business-level interpretation of internal system behavior.
