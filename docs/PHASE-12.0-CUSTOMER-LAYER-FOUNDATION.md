# Phase 12.0 — Customer Layer Foundation

Status: Proposed

## Goal

Transform ARKAVERSE from an internal operational platform into a customer-facing business website.

The objective is to create a professional public experience that introduces services, captures leads, and provides initial intelligent guidance to potential clients.

---

## Business Objective

Support:

* Business identity
* Service presentation
* Client trust building
* Lead generation
* Consultation requests

The website must be suitable for inclusion in proposals, contracts, and client communications.

---

## Customer Experience Vision

A visitor should be able to:

1. Understand who ARKAVERSE is.
2. Understand what services are offered.
3. Describe their business need.
4. Receive initial guidance.
5. Submit a consultation request.
6. Establish contact for further engagement.

---

## Website Sections

### Home

Primary business presentation.

### Services

Systemization, automation, digital transformation, operational guidance, and business intelligence services.

### About

Founder profile, expertise, experience, and methodology.

### Consultation

Customer need submission and intelligent intake process.

### Contact

Communication channels and business information.

---

## Initial Customer Flow

Visitor
↓
Service Discovery
↓
Need Description
↓
Initial Guidance
↓
Request Registration
↓
Follow-up

---

## ARKAVERSE Intelligence Role

ARKAVERSE should provide:

* Initial requirement understanding
* Preliminary operational suggestions
* Guidance before human consultation

Final recommendations remain under founder review.

---

## Architectural Direction

Existing Runtime Stack remains unchanged.

Customer Layer is added above the operational platform.

Customer Layer
↓
Business Logic
↓
Runtime Intelligence
↓
Operational Engines

No existing runtime architecture is modified.

---

## Success Criteria

A visitor can:

* Understand the business
* Understand available services
* Submit a need or request
* Receive meaningful guidance
* Become a qualified lead

---

## Outcome

Phase 12 begins the transition from internal platform development to public business experience development.
