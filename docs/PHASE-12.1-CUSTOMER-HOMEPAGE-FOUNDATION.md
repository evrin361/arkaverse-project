# Phase 12.1 — Customer Homepage Foundation

Status: Proposed

## Goal

Create the first professional customer-facing structure for ARKAVERSE.

The objective is to transform ARKAVERSE from an internal operating system into a business website that clearly communicates value to potential clients.

---

## Brand Positioning

Primary Positioning:

Architecture of Business Systems
|
Digital Transformation
|
Intelligent Organization Design

Supporting Statement:

Systemization, Intelligence and Operational Guidance for Administrative and Commercial Systems.

---

## Homepage Objectives

A visitor should understand within a few seconds:

* What ARKAVERSE is
* What problems ARKAVERSE solves
* Who ARKAVERSE serves
* How to request consultation

---

## Architectural Rule

Phase 12.1 defines information architecture only.

No advanced UI work.

No AI workflow.

No customer intelligence engine.

No backend integration.

Focus exclusively on customer-facing structure.

---

## Outcome

ARKAVERSE gains its first professional business homepage architecture and becomes ready for customer-layer implementation phases.
